welcomeScreen

Welcome screen shows the name of the game. Click anywhere to continue

mainMenu

Main menu, provide the option for the user to either start the game or exit.

Click on Play Game! to start the game.

map

Player starts with an overview map, showing all the possible stages, high score and shop.

Click on Park to start the first stage,

or click on exit if you finished the game.

Park Stage

Every stage shows the player: how many lives he has left (top left), coins he collected (top), and mails delivered so far (top right)

Player can see a mini map of the stage showing nearby mails

In park stage, player has to avoid randomly generated kites (obstacles)

Click on the bird colliding with the kite in middle to continue.

Life lost due to obstacle collision

This shows the losing state, the "owl" is falling down after colliding.

The lives on the top left decreased by one.

Click on exit to show the exit screen,

or click anywhere else to show the city stage (reachable from the map screen also)

City Stage

Second stage is the city, with buildings as an obstacles.

The player can see a mil in red between the buildings, he has to go and collect it.

Click on the timer to show the pause screen (in the game the player will press esc)

city - resume popup state

Pause screen allows the player to stop the game temporarily.

Click resume to got the mountain rock stage, or click on map and then chose the "forest" stage from there.

mountain rock

Congratulation window appears when a player clears (dilevers all the mails) any stage

Click on shop to use the coins earned, or go back to map and click on shop there.

HighScore

High score window shows the player his score comparing to the other players.

Click back to go to the map.

Shop

The shop allows the player to use his coins and unlock items that will help him to collect mails easier.

Click back to go the map screen and then click High scores from there.

exitScreen

Exit screen appears when the player loses all his lives,

it shows him his score and allow him to restart the game.

Click on testart to go back to the map.
